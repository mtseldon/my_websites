A XMas Gift The Amiga 500

For Christmas 1990, I received an Amiga 500! It was the computer I wanted for long. I couldn't believe my eyes what was standing in front of me.
and a bad surprise was waiting for me. It didn't work without a modulator and I didn't have one.
so I had to look at this box for a few more days.

After a few days, I got the modulator and finally could use my Amiga 500. It was an amazing experience to explore the capabilities of this machine. I booted to the disk and hand graphich then booted into my frist game which was North and South.

Hardware Overview

The Amiga 500 features a Motorola 68000 CPU, 512KB of RAM, and a custom chipset that allows for impressive graphics and sound capabilities for its time.

  • Motorola 68000 CPU Learn more
  • 512KB RAM (expandable to 9MB)
  • Custom OCS chipset
  • 3.5" floppy disk drive

Later a new model Amiga 1200 was released with improved specifications. I was planning to buy it but guess PC was rising
